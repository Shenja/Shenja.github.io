AR Poetry

In this exciting project in 2018 I did 3D frame-by-frame animations in a virtual space with VR-glasses, controller and a program called „NormanVR“. In augmented reality they then come to life with the help of markers. The animations refer to poems and quotes by Rupi Kaur, Charles Bukowski, Jack Kerouac und Miranda July and are supposed to visualize them.
